Vehical Management System

"A Vehicle Management System for renting and lending vehicles is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the process of managing vehicle rentals and leases. It allows users to easily browse, select, and book vehicles for rent, while also enabling vehicle owners to list their vehicles for lending. The system tracks vehicle availability, rental durations, payments, and maintenance schedules, ensuring efficient operation and user satisfaction. Additionally, it offers real-time updates, secure transactions, and detailed reports for both renters and lenders, making vehicle management seamless and convenient."
